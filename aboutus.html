Frequently Asked Questions

How much does GLOSKU cost?

Our basic software is free to use for all customers. Additional value-added features are priced pay-as-you-go.

Do you offer customer service to my international customers?

We offer local customer service as a value added service for all of our customers.

Do I need to register a company or hire a team in the destination country?

No you do not. This is how we are changing the way cross-border business is conducted. Asia has some of the most stringent regulations for foreign companies. We help you get past all of the red-tape and get started ASAP!

How do I take advantage of social e-co-mmerce?

Social e-commerce is the fastest growing sector in e-commerce. GLOSKU’s brand ambassador and affiliate program is built upon it. After setting up your account, in order to start selling your products socially; all you have to do is enable the Affiliate or Brand Ambassador programs.

Do you help us ship our products internationally?

We have partnerships with the regions’ largest logistics providers. We provide door-to-door shipping to all of our support regions.

How do I get started?

Request a demo and one of our account managers will get back to you.

Do I need to do any integration with GLOSKU?

We have integrations with most of the popular e-commerce platforms. If your platform is not yet supported or you have your own platform; please contact us to inquire about custom integrations.
